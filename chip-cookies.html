Recipe 3: Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe features delicious chocolate chip cookies. They are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a small bowl, combine the flour and baking soda. Set aside.
  3. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  4. Beat in the vanilla extract and eggs, one at a time.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the butter mixture and mix until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on baking sheets for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
